Why Get An App?

A majority of your users are accessing your forum via a smartphone. While the progressive web app suffices, the benefits of a native mobile iOS & Android app allow for increased user engagement and community growth.

Push Notifications

With native push notifications, users get alerts for comments, reactions, new posts on threads they are viewing, etc. This brings them back into the forum quickly and easily.

Easy Posting

Significantly increase new posts and comments by taking advantage of native environments and putting new posts at the forefront.

Photos & Videos

Xenforo forums suffer from an easy way to post photos/videos directly from a user's phone. This added headache for users lowers the amount of content they are posting.

Scrollability

With a native feed and the ability to show the first post of each thread, the mobile UI makes it easy for users to scroll through more posts and find content they want to engage with.

Conversations as DMs

With a native mobile app design, XF conversations are treated as direct messages, providing a more intuitive interface for private interactions.

Bespoke Development

We can add specialized features to your forum that make more sense with a mobile app, encouraging users to return and share more content.

What's the process of working together look like?
Step 1: Jump on a discovery Zoom call to go over your forum, our app solutions and talk about goals (user engagement, bespoke development, etc.)

Step 2: We make and send design mockups to go over and make changes to based on your feedback.

Step 3: Once designs are agreed to, we get to work. Building you, your own iOS and Android apps. We mostly use the API released in XF 2 but also have a custom plugin that enhances the API with additional endpoints.

How does the app get my forum data?
We access the data via the native API that XenForo released as well as through additional API endpoints that we have in our custom plugin. We never store or own your data, that continues to live on your servers. If at any time you wish to prevent the apps from accessing the data, it's as simple as revoking API keys in your admin dashboard.
How long will this take to set up?
It depends largely on how much custom development we need to do for your specific forum -- but we think we have a very modern and intuitive starting point. If you have very unique requests (i.e. non XenForo native features) we'll have to figure out a way to include those.
